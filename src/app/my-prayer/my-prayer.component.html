*FORMAT

I pray [your personal prayer]

I pray for [your personal prayer]

*TEXT GUIDE

  • Refrain from submitting vague statements/ prayers
  • Descriptive prayers are highly encouraged but do not hesitate to submit simple prayers too.
  • For a more elaborate output, feel free to describe your prayers.
    • Sample: I pray for a two-storey house by the beach, modern architecture, painted white, calm waves, beautiful garden in front.
  • Avoid using specific names or other private details (home address, phone number, etc.)
    • Sample Identifier: A person, man, woman, mother, father, boy, girl, etc.
    • A General location: home, city, etc.
  • Prayers must be for good (i.e. not disrespectful, no sexual or violent content, etc.)
  • Don't forget to have fun and pray from the heart.
